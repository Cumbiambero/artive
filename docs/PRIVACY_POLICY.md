# Privacy Policy for Artive

**Last updated: January 29, 2026**

## Overview

Artive is an artwork cataloguing application designed for visual artists. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use Artive.

## Data Storage

Artive stores your data in **your personal Supabase project**. This means:

- **You own your data** - All artwork metadata and images are stored in a cloud database that you create and control
- **We have no access** - The developers of Artive cannot access your data
- **You can delete it anytime** - Simply delete your Supabase project to remove all data

## What Data is Stored

When you use Artive, the following data is stored in your Supabase project:

- Artwork metadata (titles, descriptions, dates, dimensions, medium)
- Images of your artworks
- Your Supabase credentials are stored locally on your device only

## Data Collection by Artive

**Artive does not collect any data.** Specifically:

- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No crash reporting
- ❌ No advertising
- ❌ No user accounts on our servers
- ❌ No data transmission to Artive developers

## Third-Party Services

Artive uses **Supabase** as the backend service for data storage. When you create a Supabase account and project:

- Supabase may collect account information you provide to them
- Please review [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details

## Permissions

Artive requests the following device permissions:

- **Camera**: To photograph your artworks
- **Photo Library**: To select existing photos of your artworks
- **Internet**: To sync data with your Supabase project

## Children's Privacy

Artive is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app's repository.
